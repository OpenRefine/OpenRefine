Design patterns
Due Tuesday by 4pm Points 10 Submitting a file upload File Types pdf Available after May 12 at 4pm
As a team, identify five different design patterns inside your system.  At least two of the patterns that you identify should be behavioral in nature.  Please highlight where in the system each design pattern exists, discuss whether it is used exactly as it is supposed to or whether the code deviates from the exact definition, and describe why you think the developers used it for that particular part of the system (what benefits does it provide in this context).

Note, the Singleton, Iterator, and Factory Method patterns are not eligible.

To Canvas, submit a brief document that includes the following, per issue and associate pull request:

title
a link to the issue
a link to your pull request
